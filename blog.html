5 Signs Your Irrigation System Needs an Upgrade

An inefficient irrigation system can waste thousands of gallons of water and leave your lawn looking patchy. Watch for these warning signs: uneven watering, soggy spots, rising water bills, sprinkler heads not rotating, or dry zones despite regular watering.
